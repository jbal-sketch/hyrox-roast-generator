Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?

Yes! The Hyrox Roast Generator is completely free. Just paste your results URL and get roasted!

Do you store my personal information or race results?

No, we don't permanently store any of your data. Your results are processed in real-time to generate your roast and are not saved on our servers. We respect your privacy!

What if my results URL doesn't work?

Make sure your URL is from hyresult.com and includes your result ID. The URL should look like: https://www.hyresult.com/result/YOUR_RESULT_ID. If you're still having issues, try adding ?tab=splits to the end of your URL.

Are the roasts mean-spirited or offensive?

Absolutely not! Our roasts are designed to be funny, playful, and light-hearted - like friendly banter between training partners. They're meant to celebrate your achievement while having a laugh. We never create mean or hurtful content.

Can I use my roast on social media?

Yes! That's the whole point! Share your roast on Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, or wherever you want. Just make sure to tag us @roastmyhyrox so we can see it!

How accurate is the data extraction?

We do our best to extract all relevant data from your results page, including times, positions, and splits. However, if some data is missing from the page, we'll work with what's available. The roast will still be generated based on the data we can access.

Can I generate multiple roasts?

Sure! You can generate roasts for as many different race results as you want. Each roast is unique and based on that specific race's performance data.

What if I want to report an issue or provide feedback?

We'd love to hear from you! Reach out to us on Instagram @roastmyhyrox or through our GitHub repository. Your feedback helps us improve!
